MemoryVerse

Learn a passage by rebuilding it one missing word at a time.

Choose a verse or verse range, pick a translation, and each cleared round hides one more random word until you type the whole passage from memory.

How it works

One random word hides to start. Clear the hidden words in order to add another.

If you miss a word, that word reappears and you restart the round on the rest.
